How to Play Shaki Spoons:

  1. Click "Start Sound App" to begin
  2. Allow motion sensor access when prompted - this enables the core shake-to-play feature!
  3. Shake your phone to play shaki spoon sounds (the main feature!)
  4. Tap the large spoon or anywhere on screen as an alternative
  5. Select different spoon types for authentic shaki sounds
  6. Adjust the Playing Tempo slider to change rhythm patterns and speed
  7. Use your device's volume controls to adjust sound level
  8. Try rapid shaking for shaki-style rhythms

Shaki Spoon Types:

🥄 Wooden Spoon: Traditional Appalachian sound - warm, earthy tones perfect for old-time shaki
🥄 Metal Spoon: Bright, cutting sound - ideal for fast-paced shaki breakdowns
🥄 Plastic Spoon: Modern shaki sound - clean, consistent rhythm for contemporary styles
🥄 Ceramic Spoon: Resonant, bell-like tones - perfect for waltz-time shaki ballads
