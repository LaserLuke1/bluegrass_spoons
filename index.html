How to Play Shaki Spoons:

  1. Click "Start Sound App" to begin
  2. Allow motion and orientation sensor access when prompted - this enables shake-to-play and rotation-controlled effects!
  3. Shake your phone to play shaki spoon sounds (the main feature!)
  4. Rotate your device to control effects in real-time:
    • Rotate left/right (Yaw): Controls delay time
    • Tilt left/right (Roll): Controls reverb room size
    • Tilt forward/back (Pitch): Controls overdrive amount
  5. Tap the large spoon or anywhere on screen as an alternative
  6. Select different spoon types to hear different sounds
  7. Adjust the Playing Tempo slider to change rhythm patterns - from sparse hits to rapid arpeggiated cascades
  8. Use your device's volume controls to adjust sound level
  9. Try rapid shaking for shaki-style rhythms with rotation-controlled effects!
